Accomidation FAQ's

Can I reserve accommodation and buy seva tickets through this site?
The facility of online reservation of accommodation and seva tickets please visit www.ttdsevaonline.com. You can reserve Special Entry/Accommodation tickets 90 days from Quota availability.Seva quota will be released first Friday of every month and Piligrim can book seva tickets after 180 days of booking date.
What type of cards accepted in internet booking ?
Pilgrims can avail TTD e-Services(Seva, Darshan, Accommodation, Sapthagiri Magazine, Hundi & Donations) using all Master and Visa Debit / Credit Cards and Net Banking facility. Foreign Cards and Mastero cards containing 20 digit numbers are not accepted.

RouteMap FAQ's

Problems during Payment Process in internet booking ?
Some times in view of late response from the payment gateway, the transaction will not be processed successfully. If any failure transaction and amount got debited from the account the same will be refunded with in 7 working days In some cases due to technical issue the transaction will not be processed and the transaction status will have "Waiting for payment Gateway Response". In this case, the transactions will be automatically be successed in case the amount got debited from the pilgrims account most of the cases. The transaction status "Ticket released after a period of inactivity" shall be treated as failure transaction if the transaction amount got debitted from the pilgrim account the same will be refunded within 7 working days
