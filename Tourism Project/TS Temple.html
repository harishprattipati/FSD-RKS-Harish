Bias alleged in fund allocation to Basara temple


BASARA : Devotees have charged the State government with allocating huge sums to Yadadri and Vemulavada temples and discriminating Basara Saraswati temple.

The devotees are appealing to the government to release funds to implement the master plan for Basara temple development and correct Vastu defects. It may be recalled that noted architect Anand Sai has prepared a plan for development of the temple.

Thousand Pillar temple to get facelift; KUDA to acquire land


The Kakatiya Urban Development Authority (KUDA), which has already launched beautification works under the national Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana (HRIDAY) scheme at the historical Thousand Pillar temple, is planning to acquire nearly 2.5 acres of land near the temple in an attempt to add elegance to the temple.



KUDA authorities have already shot off a letter to the district Collector urging her to take steps for the land acquisition.

Huge rush at Vemulawada temple


Even as the Sankranti holidays have come to close, pilgrims thronged the Sri Raja Rajeshwara Swamy Devasthanam in Vemulawada on Friday. Devotees formed serpentine queues for darshan of the presiding deities and also to offer traditional “Kode Mokku” (tying of an ox) at the temple.


Yadagirigutta temple gets ISO certification


In a first in Telangana, Lord Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy temple located at Yadadri got ISO certification. The ISO certification was given for the temple on environment, hygiene, security, management and power supply aspects. The ISO team visited the temple, which is currently under renovation, last Saturday. Speaking to Express, temple executive officer N Geetha said the ISO team had expressed satisfaction over the environs, where the sculpting work was going on.

Want a US visa? Head to Hyderabad's Chilkur Balaji temple to make it happen!


New Delhi: Amazing! The famed Chilkur Balaji temple in Hyderabad can help fulfill all your wishes, including granting of US and other visas, devotees claim.

According to a media report, scores of devotees, planning to visit the US, make it a point to visit this ancient temple to help grant American visas.

The Chilkur Balaji temple, built as a shrine to Lord Balaji, is said to be about 500 years old.

Chennakesava Swamy temple fete concludes


It was curtains down for the annual Brahmotsavam at the Sridevi Bhoodevi Sametha Prasanna Chennakesava Swamy temple with performance of ‘Chakrasnanam’ on Tuesday.

The ‘Sudarsana Chakra’, the Lord’s disc, was immersed in holy waters amid chanting of Vedic hymns by priests led by Parankusa Ramanadhacharyulu and Narayanam Srinivasa Deekshitulu. The sacred flag hoisted atop the ‘Dhwaja Sthambam’ on the first day of the festival in honour of Lord Chennakesavaswamy, an incarnation of Lord Sriman Narayana, was lowered.

Devotees from far and near pulled the beautifully decorated chariot late on Monday amidst chants of Govinda, Govinda. Children hogged the limelight during the musical nite held by Lalita Kala Academy president Santaveluri Koteswara Rao.
